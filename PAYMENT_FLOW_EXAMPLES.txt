╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    PAYMENT STATUS MEANINGS                        ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

🔴 UNPAID
   ├─ No payment received yet
   ├─ Full amount pending
   ├─ Usually for credit customers
   └─ Collect full amount on delivery

🟡 ADVANCE PAID
   ├─ Partial advance received
   ├─ Remaining amount pending
   ├─ Most common scenario
   └─ Collect remaining on delivery

🟠 PARTIALLY PAID
   ├─ Some payment received
   ├─ More than advance, less than full
   ├─ Multiple installments
   └─ Collect remaining balance

🟢 FULLY PAID
   ├─ Complete payment received
   ├─ No pending amount
   ├─ Nothing to collect
   └─ Just deliver the goods

╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    COMMON QUESTIONS                              ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Q: What if customer pays more advance later?
A: Update the assignment with new advance amount and change status
   to "Partially Paid" if not yet full.

Q: Can I change payment status after assignment?
A: Yes, you can edit the assignment in the database or create an
   update feature (future enhancement).

Q: What if payment is in multiple parts?
A: Use "Partially Paid" status and update advance amount as each
   payment is received.

Q: How does vendor see this information?
A: Vendor sees payment details in their "Assigned Fleets" screen
   in the mobile app.

Q: Can vendor update payment status?
A: Currently no. Only internal team can update. This ensures
   payment tracking accuracy.

Q: What currency is used?
A: Indian Rupees (₹) by default. Amounts are stored as decimal
   numbers in the database.
