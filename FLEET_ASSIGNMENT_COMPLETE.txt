HOW IT WORKS:
------------

FOR INTERNAL TEAM:
1. Open fleet_assignment_panel.php in browser
2. Select vehicle from dropdown (shows vendor name, driver, online status)
3. Enter AL number (e.g., AL123456)
4. Enter pickup and delivery locations
5. Set expected completion date
6. Add optional notes
7. Click "Assign Fleet" button
8. Assignment is created and vendor can see it in their app

FOR VENDORS:
1. Open vendor app
2. Click "Assigned" button on dashboard
3. See two tabs: Active and Completed
4. View all fleet assignments with:
   - AL number
   - Vehicle and driver details
   - Pickup/delivery locations
   - Assignment date and expected completion
   - Status badge
   - Notes from internal team
5. Pull down to refresh
